03 — Tax Efficiency

Dean's US Tax Position

Option B is structurally superior on tax. The US-UK Double Taxation Convention eliminates UK exposure on Dean's consultancy income entirely.

Income Type UK Tax? US Tax? Treaty Basis Net Effect
Option A: Dividends from UK Ltd Yes — withholding Yes — worldwide Art. 10 (partial relief only) True double taxation risk
Option B: US consultancy fees from Ellis's UK Ltd No — zero Yes — federal/state Art. 7 Business Profits — no UK permanent establishment Single taxation only

💡
Key Principle: US-Sourced Income When Dean performs marketing services from the United States — regardless of who the client is — that income is classified as US-sourced income. The UK has no taxing right under Article 7 of the US-UK Tax Treaty because Dean has no "permanent establishment" in the UK. He simply declares the income on his US federal return (Schedule C or through a US business entity). No UK tax forms, no UK withholding, no double taxation.

06 — Contract Framework

Key Contractual Protections for Dean

The Marketing Services Agreement is Dean's primary protection document. These provisions must be included as non-negotiable terms.

1

Revenue-Linked Fee (Not Discretionary Retainer)

Dean's fee must be expressed as a fixed percentage of whatever the Manchester CMC pays Ellis's company — not a flat retainer that Ellis can reduce or cancel at will. If Ellis earns more, Dean earns more automatically.

Suggested: 65–70% of AR revenue received
2

IP Ownership of the Database Remains with Dean

The Wincham database is Dean's intellectual property. The Data Licence Agreement runs directly between Dean and the Manchester CMC — not through Ellis's company. Ellis cannot cancel this or use it as leverage.

Data Licence: Dean → Manchester CMC (direct)
3

Non-Circumvention Clause

Neither Ellis nor the Manchester CMC may approach the other party to cut Dean out of the commercial arrangement. Breach triggers full damages equal to the revenue Dean would have received for the contracted term.

Minimum 3-year non-circumvention period
4

Fees Survive Structural Changes

If Ellis's UK company is wound up, restructured, or if the AR arrangement with the Manchester CMC changes, Dean's marketing fee obligation transfers to any successor entity or flows directly from the Manchester CMC.

Assignment clause protecting Dean's income stream
5

Defined Scope — Marketing Services Only

Dean's contractual scope must be carefully defined as marketing services (campaigns, content, analytics, outreach strategy) — explicitly NOT seeking out claimants or referring claimants. This maintains the regulatory boundary correctly.

Regulatory boundary clearly drafted
6

GDPR Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs)

Where Dean handles any personal data of UK Wincham victims, Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) must be appended to the agreement to lawfully govern transfer of personal data from the UK to the US.

Boilerplate SCCs — minimal cost to implement
