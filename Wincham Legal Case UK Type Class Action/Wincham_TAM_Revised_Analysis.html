2.2 Head 2 — Corporate Banking Failure (Proceeds in Wrong Account)

Head 2 is directly and materially property-value-dependent. The loss is: - Additional tax cost = (Dividend tax rate 39.35% − Correct CGT rate 24%) × net sale proceeds received into personal account - Lost FTCR = Spanish CGT already paid, now unrecoverable because personal receipt negates corporate CGT treatment - Professional rescue costs = MVL restructuring, tax specialist advice, HMRC correspondence

This head only applies to victims who have sold or are selling. Based on the scheme's history (operating since mid-1990s) and the ATED pressures accelerating exits, we estimate 40–60% of the 782 victim companies have already sold or are in the process of selling.
