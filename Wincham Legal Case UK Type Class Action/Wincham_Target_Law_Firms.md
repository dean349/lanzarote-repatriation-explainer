## TIER 3 — CLAIMS MANAGEMENT COMPANY (CMC) ROUTE

### CMC Option: FCA-Authorised Claims Management Company

| Field | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Technical Regulatory Name** | Claims Management Company (CMC) |
| **Regulator** | Financial Conduct Authority (FCA) — regulation took effect April 2019 under the Financial Guidance and Claims Act 2018 |
| **FCA Register** | register.fca.org.uk — filter for "Claims Management" authorisation |
| **Relevant Authorisation Category** | "Financial products and services" |

**Important distinction:** CMCs **cannot litigate**. They identify, qualify, and refer claimants to solicitors — identical in function to what your playbook describes. The risk of approaching a CMC is they may become a competitor, not a client.

> [!WARNING]
> Do NOT operate as an unregistered CMC. If you are commercially referring individuals to law firms in exchange for payment, the FCA may require CMC authorisation. Ensure Ellis Harrison's entity remains within the "introducer" exemption (covered by the Introduction Fee Agreement already drafted) and does not cross into the regulated CMC definition.
