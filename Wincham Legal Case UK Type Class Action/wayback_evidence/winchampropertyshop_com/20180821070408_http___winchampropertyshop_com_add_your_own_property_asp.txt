No Yes The Ministry of Industry has set the regulations, that it is obligatory for all properties that are advertised for sale in Spain must have an "Energy Performance Certificate". If a property is advertised for sale that does not hold an EPC the owner/s of the property will be fined up to &euro;6000 if identified by the Spanish Goverment. The costs per certificate are:- Up to 100m2 - &euro;155 + IVA Between 100m2-200m2 - &euro;195 + IVA Between 200m2 - 300m2 - &euro;215 + IVA Up to 400m2 - &euro;280 + IVA Over 400m2 - Please speak to Agent.
